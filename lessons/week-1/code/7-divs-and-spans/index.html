div and span practice

Try out using divs and spans yourself.

  • In your boring-lecture.html page, remove the intro class from the elements themselves, and create a <div> with the intro class to hold the elements.
  • On your dog's blog, use <div>s to group together and style each blog post.
  • On your cat's homepage, create a <div> for an "about me" section that's styled differently from the rest of the page.
  • Update your favorite things page and your first webpage with some spans to style specific parts of text.
