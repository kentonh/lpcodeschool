Box model practice

Here are some exercises for you to practice using margins, borders, and padding:

  • For the sidebar to your dog's blog, add a border and a bit of padding and margin. Give the sidebar a background color.
  • Between each entry on your dog's blog, add a bit of space. Use margin-top and margin-bottom so that you don't increase the spacing on the sides.
  • Add some padding around the images on your cat's homepage.
  • Give different background colors to each column of your favorite things.
