Styling with Classes practice

Try out using classes yourself:

  • In your my-first-webpage.html, pick the three most interesting elements, give them a class called flashy, and create a CSS rule to make them red and italic.
  • In your favorite-things.html, give a class called best to your number one favorites in each category, and apply a style of your choice to make them stand out.
  • Add some styles of your own choosing to your cat's homepage and your dog's blog.
  • Create a new page called boring-lecture.html that is a fake page about a boring subject of your choice. Use lorem ipsum text to fill up the page. Create a section of the page at the beginning for an intro to the lecture; in that intro, include a subheading, a paragraph or two, and a picture. Give each of these elements the class intro. Then, create CSS rules for h2.intro, p.intro, and img.intro.
