Putting all the pieces together

This week we'll be using everything we've learned so far to build a Quote Quiz. Pick any two people, and find 4-5 quotes from both. Use HTML, CSS, and Javascript to make website. This page will show a quote and test our visior's knowledge of who said it.

  • When a visitor lands on the page, present one quote with two buttons.
  • When a visitor clicks on their answer, show them correct or incorrect.
  • Give the option to view another quote, and when clicked laod a new quote for the user to guess on.
  • If you finish that, improve the design of your app.
  • If you finish that, animate the correct/incorrect.
  • If you finish that, animate the new questions.
